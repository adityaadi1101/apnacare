APNA CARE HOSPITAL

Aashi Care Hospital is a multi-speciality hospital with high-end diagnostic and treatment facilities. Located at Panki Road Redma, Daltonganj, Jharkhand. The Hospital is designed to have a continuum of safe and high-quality care for health and well-being. Aashi Care Hospital brings cutting-edge technology to compliment the skills of doctors and para medical staff.

Aashi Care Hospital provides 24/7 emergency service, with specialized Major OT, Minor OT, ICU, Ultrasound, General wards, Deluxe, and Super Deluxe fully air condotioned private cabin wards, Dialysis Unit, Pharmacy, High-End Laboratory, USG, and Digital X-ray etc. Round the clock Doctors 24 hours Ambulance and all types of Insurance and Govt Medical Schemes, In-House Canteen and parking facility available.

We have specialties like Anesthesiology, Diabetics & Endocrinology. Dermatology, ENT, General surgery, Internal Medicine, General Medicine, Obstetrics and Gynecology (OBG), Minimum Invasive Surgery, Nephrology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation, Radiology, Urology, Trauma, etc.
